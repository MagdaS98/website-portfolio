WELCOME TO MENTAL PARK

We are a group of dedicated fans of Linkin Park and our mission is to spread mental health awareness and help people who feel trapped.

We also organise many events with good music and discussions! We believe the world can be a better place.

In case you or someone you know needs support, here are some resources:

Suicide Prevention Lifeline 1-800-273-TALK

Crisis Text Line, the free, nationwide, 24/7 text message service for people in crisis, is here to support.

For support in the United States, text HELLO to 741741 or message at facebook.com/CrisisTextLine.

For support outside the US, find resources at http://www.suicide.org/international-suicide-hotlines.html

If you feel like you or someone close to you need to talk and let something out, our group is here for you.
