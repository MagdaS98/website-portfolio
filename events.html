MENTAL HEALTH EVENTS

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood. We organise one event about Mental Health Awareness every two months in London where you can listen to our special guests that always give good advice and tips on how to take care of your well-being.
