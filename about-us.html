ABOUT US

We are a group of dedicated fans of Linkin Park and our mission is to spread mental health awareness and help people who feel alone with their problems. We started our community in 2017 after Chester, one o the member of Linkin Park, died by commiting suicide. We decided to join together and as a family, support each other and other people who fight constant battles with their mental health. Also, we started spreading awareness about mental health through our magazine and events.

Apart from propagating mental health, we also organise events dedicated to Linkin Park and their music. It is hard not to be sad that the band stopped producing music and playing concerts, therefore our events are the perfect opportunity to have fun to their songs.

We believe that the world can be a better place. Join our community and help us spreading mental health awareness and good music!
